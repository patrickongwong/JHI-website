About JHI

Januarius Holdings, Inc. ("JHI") was formed in 2008 to manage the personal assets of its Principal, Januario Jesus B. Atencio.

Mr. Atencio built a distinguished 25-year career in the Philippine mass housing industry, culminating in his decade-long tenure as President and CEO of 8990 Holdings, Inc. (PSE: HOUSE)—the country’s largest and most successful mass housing developer. Under his leadership, HOUSE achieved industry-leading growth and attracted institutional investors, such as TPG and Khazanah Nasional Berhad, in its 2014 IPO.

Following his retirement and the sale of his shares of HOUSE in 2017, JJ formally structured JHI into a family office which has since managed a portfolio of more than USD100M in assets across Real Estate, Private Equity, Capital Markets and Art.

Through JHI, Mr. Atencio is also a key sponsor of the Ateneo Blue Eagles as well as the title sponsor of the largest golf tournament in the Philippines, the Januarius Fil-Am Invitational.
